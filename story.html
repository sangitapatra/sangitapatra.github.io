Our Story

Aug 1, 2007

How We Met

It was the time when we had just passed out from our respective schools and coindidentally decided to take admission on the same day, at the same college pursuing the same degree.

That was the very first time we met. But all throughout college we remained very good friends and nothing more. But like it's said, if it has to happen it will happen. And so it did... eventually...

The Search

My parents decided to sign me up on one of those matrimonial portals to find a prospective life partner. Pretty cliche right? Yes I know.... :(

While browsing thorugh the endless list of prospective brides, I found a dear friend from college. We chatted for a while, and realized things could lead to something better. Chats gave way to extended phone calls and eventually to that day...

It was our first date. While sitting and chatting on a not so cold winter afternoon at a rooftop restaurant in the heart of Kolkata, we didn't realise how soon time flew.

First Movie

Sangita is an avid SRK fan. Me not so much. So when she wanted to watch Dilwale, I was really not looking forward to the movie at all. But well it our first of something, so I decided to go along with it.

The movie was crap, the storyline was even weirder, but I was happy. I had a gorgeous girl right next to me. I was in love. She was too. That's all that mattered.

And The Big Decision

The decision was made. I had found my better half. She had too. You know the feeling, when you crave for an ice-cream or chocolate or biryani, for instance? We had a craving too. We wanted to get married. I know it sounds silly. But that's the way it was. That's our love story.

Our parents met. Decided the dates. And here we are inviting you all to our big day.
